Which type of milk is best for you?

Jonathan J. O'Sullivan & Grace E. Cunningham
If you go to the store in search of milk, there are a dizzying number of products to choose from. 如果你去商店寻找奶类产品，琳琅满目的选择多到让人眼花缭乱。
There’s dairy milk, but also plant-based products. 除了动物牛奶，还有各种植物基产品。
To turn a plant into something resembling milk, it must be either soaked, drained, rinsed, and milled into a thick paste, or dried, and milled into flour. 要把植物变成类似奶的状态，必须经过浸泡、沥干、冲洗并磨成浓稠的浆糊，或者将其干燥后磨成粉。
The plant paste or flour is then fortified with vitamins and minerals, flavoured, and diluted with water. 接着，在植物浆或植物粉中强化维生素和矿物质，调味并用水稀释。
The result is a barrage of options that share many of the qualities of animal milk. 其结果是产生了一系列具有动物奶特性的替代选择。
So which milk is actually best for you? Let’s dive into some of the most popular milks: dairy, almond, soy, or oat? 那么，究竟哪种奶对你最好？让我们深入研究最受欢迎的几种奶：牛奶、杏仁奶、豆奶或燕麦奶？
A 250 ml glass of cow’s milk contains 8 grams of protein, 12 grams of carbohydrates, and 2 to 8 grams of fat depending on if it’s skim, reduced fat, or whole. 一杯 250 毫升的牛奶含有 8 克蛋白质、12 克碳水化合物和 2 到 8 克脂肪，具体取决于它是脱脂、低脂还是全脂奶。
That’s approximately 15% the daily protein an average adult needs, roughly 10% the carbohydrates and 2 to 15% the fat. 这大约占成人每日所需蛋白质的 15%，碳水化合物的 10% 以及脂肪的 2% 到 15%。
Most plant-based milks have less carbohydrates than dairy milk. 大多数植物奶的碳水化合物含量低于牛奶。
They also have less fat, but more of what’s often called “good fats.” 脂肪也更少，但含有更多所谓的“优质脂肪”。
Meanwhile, the healthy nutrients vitamin D and calcium found in dairy milk don’t occur naturally in most plant-based milks. 同时，牛奶中含有的健康营养素维生素 D 和钙在大多数植物奶中并非天然存在。
Looking more closely at our plant-based milks, both almond and oat are low in protein compared to dairy. 仔细观察植物奶，杏仁奶和燕麦奶的蛋白质含量都低于牛奶。
But while almond milk has the least nutrients of the four, oat milk is full of beta-glucans, a healthy type of fibre. 虽然杏仁奶是四种奶中营养最少的，但燕麦奶富含 β-葡聚糖，这是一种健康的纤维。
It also has a lot of carbohydrates compared to other plant milks — sometimes as much as dairy milk. 与其他植物奶相比，它的碳水化合物含量也较高，有时甚至与牛奶相当。
Soy milk, meanwhile, has as much protein as cow’s milk and is also a great source of potassium. 同时，豆奶的蛋白质含量与牛奶相当，也是钾的极好来源。
Soybeans contain isoflavone, which people used to think might trigger hormonal imbalances by mimicking the function of estrogen. 大豆含有异黄酮，人们过去认为它可能通过模拟雌激素的功能引发激素失衡。
But ultimately, soy milk contains very small amounts of isoflavones, which have a much weaker effect on our bodies than estrogen. 但最终研究表明，豆奶中的异黄酮含量极低，对人体的影响远弱于雌激素。
Depending on individual circumstances, one of these milks may be the clear winner. 根据个人情况，其中一种奶可能会成为明显的胜出者。
If you’re lactose intolerant, then the plant-based milks pull ahead, while if you’re allergic to nuts, almond milk is out. 如果你有乳糖不耐受，植物奶就更胜一筹；如果你对坚果过敏，杏仁奶就被排除在外。
For people who don’t have access to a wide and varied diet, dairy milk can be the most efficient way to get these nutrients. 对于无法获得多样化饮食的人来说，牛奶是获取这些营养素最有效的方式。
But all else being equal, any one of these four milks is nutritious enough to be part of a balanced diet. 但在其他条件相同的情况下，这四种奶中的任何一种都足够营养，可以作为均衡饮食的一部分。
That’s why for many people, the milk that’s best for you is actually the milk that’s best for the planet. 这就是为什么对许多人来说，最适合你的奶实际上是对地球最好的奶。
So which uses the fewest resources and produces the least pollution? 那么，哪种奶消耗的资源最少、产生的污染最低呢？
It takes almost 4 square kilometers to produce just one glass of cow’s milk, land use that drives deforestation and habitat destruction. 生产一小杯牛奶需要占用近 4 平方公里的土地，这种土地使用会导致森林砍伐和栖息地破坏。
Most of that is land the cows live on, and some is used to grow their feed. Many cows eat soy beans and oats. 其中大部分是奶牛居住的土地，还有一部分用于种植饲料；许多奶牛吃大豆和燕麦。
It takes much less land to grow the oats or soybeans for milk than it does to feed a dairy cow — only about a quarter square kilometer per glass. 种植用于制奶的燕麦或大豆所需的土地远少于喂养奶牛所需的土地——每杯奶仅需约 0.25 平方公里。
Almond milk has similar land use. But where that land is also matters. 杏仁奶的土地使用量与之相似。但土地所在的位置也很重要。
Soybean farms are a major driver of deforestation, while oat and almond farms are n’t. 大豆农场是森林砍伐的主要驱动力，而燕麦和杏仁农场则不是。
Making milk uses water every step of the way, but it’s the farming stage where big differences emerge. 制奶的每一步都要消耗水，但在种植/养殖阶段出现了巨大的差异。
Dairy milk uses the most water — about 120 liters per glass, mostly to water cows and grow their food. 牛奶耗水最多，每杯约 120 升，主要用于给奶牛饮用和种植饲料。
Almonds take second place, at more than 70 liters of water per glass. 杏仁奶位居第二，每杯耗水超过 70 升。
Most of that water is used to grow almond trees, which take years of watering before they start producing almonds. 这些水大部分用于种植杏仁树，杏仁树需要多年的浇灌才能开始结果。
The trees must be watered consistently, or they die, while many other crops can be left fallow and still produce later. 杏仁树必须持续浇水，否则会枯死；而许多其他作物可以休耕并在以后继续产出。
All told, soy and oats require less water to grow: only about 5 to 10 liters per glass of milk. 总的来说，大豆和燕麦生长所需的水较少：每杯奶仅需约 5 到 10 升。
Milk production generates some greenhouse gas emissions — about 0.1 to 0.2 kilograms per glass for the plant-based milks. 牛奶生产会产生温室气体排放——植物奶每杯约为 0.1 到 0.2 公斤。
But for dairy milk, the cows themselves also produce emissions by burping and farting out large quantities of the gas methane. 但对于牛奶来说，奶牛自身也会通过打嗝和排气排出大量的甲烷气体。
Overall, each glass of dairy milk contributes over half a kilogram of greenhouse gas emissions. 总的来说，每杯牛奶产生的温室气体排放超过半公斤。
So while depending on your dietary needs, any one of these milks may be a good fit, in terms of the health of our planet there’s a strong case for choosing plant-based milks, particularly oat or soy milk. 因此，虽然根据你的饮食需求，这些奶中的任何一种都可能适合，但从地球健康的角度来看，选择植物奶（尤其是燕麦奶或豆奶）的理由非常充分。
