Which type of milk is best for you?

Jonathan J. O'Sullivan & Grace E. Cunningham

If you go to the store in search of milk, there are a dizzying number of products to choose from. There’s dairy milk, but also plant-based products. 如果你去商店寻找奶，琳琅满目的产品多到让人眼花缭乱。除了牛奶，还有各种植物基产品。
To turn a plant into something resembling milk, it must be either soaked, drained, rinsed, and milled into a thick paste, or dried, and milled into flour. 要把植物变成类似奶的状态，必须经过浸泡、沥干、冲洗并磨成浓稠的浆糊，或者将其干燥后磨成粉。
The plant paste or flour is then fortified with vitamins and minerals, flavoured, and diluted with water. The result is a barrage of options that share many of the qualities of animal milk. 接着，在植物浆或植物粉中强化维生素和矿物质，调味并用水稀释。其结果是产生了一系列具有动物奶特性的替代选择。
So which milk is actually best for you? Let’s dive into some of the most popular milks: dairy, almond, soy, or oat? 那么，究竟哪种奶对你最好？让我们深入研究最受欢迎的几种奶：牛奶、杏仁奶、豆奶或燕麦奶？
A 250 ml glass of cow’s milk contains 8 grams of protein, 12 grams of carbohydrates, and 2 to 8 grams of fat. That’s approximately 15% the daily protein an average adult needs. 一杯 250 毫升的牛奶含有 8 克蛋白质、12 克碳水化合物和 2 到 8 克脂肪。这大约是成人每日所需蛋白质的 15%。
Most plant-based milks have less carbohydrates than dairy milk. They also have less fat, but more of what’s often called “good fats.” 大多数植物奶的碳水化合物含量低于牛奶。脂肪也更少，但含有更多所谓的“优质脂肪”。
Looking more closely at our plant-based milks, both almond and oat are low in protein compared to dairy. But oat milk is full of beta-glucans, a healthy type of fibre. 仔细观察植物奶，杏仁奶和燕麦奶的蛋白质含量都低于牛奶。但燕麦奶富含β-葡聚糖，这是一种健康的纤维。
Soy milk, meanwhile, has as much protein as cow’s milk and is also a great source of potassium. Soybeans contain isoflavone, which we now know has a much weaker effect on our bodies than estrogen. 豆奶的蛋白质含量与牛奶相当，也是钾的极好来源。大豆含有异黄酮，我们现在知道它对人体的影响远弱于雌激素。
It takes almost 4 square kilometers to produce just one glass of cow’s milk, land use that drives deforestation and habitat destruction. 生产一小杯牛奶需要占用近 4 平方公里的土地，这种土地使用导致了森林砍伐和栖息地破坏。
Dairy milk uses the most water — about 120 liters per glass. Almonds take second place at more than 70 liters. Soy and oats only require about 5 to 10 liters. 牛奶耗水最多，每杯约 120 升。杏仁奶位居第二，耗水超过 70 升。而豆奶和燕麦奶仅需约 5 到 10 升。
Overall, each glass of dairy milk contributes over half a kilogram of greenhouse gas emissions, about three times more than plant-based milks. 总的来说，每杯牛奶产生的温室气体排放超过半公斤，约为植物奶的三倍。
In terms of the health of our planet, there’s a strong case for choosing plant-based milks, particularly oat or soy milk. 从地球健康的角度来看，选择植物奶（尤其是燕麦奶或豆奶）的理由非常充分。
